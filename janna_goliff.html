Janna Goliff

Examining our relationship with memory, documentation, and space, Goliff’s project recreates the intimate space of her bedroom for any curious viewers to explore. Skeptical of virtual reality, she also uses the project as a generative opportunity to explore how a full immersion of the personal with the virtual can affect our understanding of the world. In asserting her space’s value through meticulous documentation, she hopes to challenge what we deem worthy of making into a digital experience.
