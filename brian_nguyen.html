Strictly Funk Dance Company is the premier hip-hop and contemporary dance group at the University of Pennsylvania. The company was formed in 1997 with the intention of transcending the traditional boundaries of dance. Throughout its years of existence, Funk has gained a following on Penn’s campus and has evolved since then into a creative production company with the goal of having no limits.

This year will be Strictly Funk’s 25th anniversary and as an homage and celebration to the organization that has shaped many lives, the Hall of Funk serves as an archival and visual exploration of the past 25 years with a produced documentary that narrates the past 25 years of Funk’s existence. Let’s funk.
