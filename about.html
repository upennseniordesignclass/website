In Search Of, the first Senior Design Exhibition at the University of Pennsylvania brings together our interdisciplinary studies throughout the past four years as part of the new Design major. In this exhibition, some students reflect on memory, considering our culture and heritage, the communities, music, foods, and objects that have become a part of who we are, through explorations across augmented and virtual reality, website design, and fabrication. Playful practices of documentation and speculation allow others to critically examine contemporary technologies, from social media to surveillance tools, and to consider their place within our lives. Handmade publications and a lighting installation engage viewers to interact with them, and other works concern the urban environment and ponder ways in which we can promote sustainability.
